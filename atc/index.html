Air Traffic Controller Career

A High-Stakes, High-Reward Path with the US FAA

Prep Tips from Successful Candidates

  • Intense Practice: Use specialized prep software (like JobTestPrep or TSATestPrep) for simulations. Many find it boosts confidence and scores.
  • Mental Math: Practice fast subtraction/addition without paper (e.g., 87 - 34).
  • Prioritize: In the radar simulation, always prioritize avoiding collisions (90% of score) over math pop-ups.
  • Stay Calm: The test is designed to be overwhelming. Focus on recovering quickly from mistakes.
